About

Cooper Norman

I'm a founder-engineer who builds production systems end-to-end, from data pipelines and ML models to live serving, safety rails, and the operational glue that keeps it all running. Most of that came from building ShareShark solo: a concurrency-safe money ledger, an ACH payout and KYC/AML/tax compliance stack, a calibrated LightGBM options-pricing engine, a suite of cooperating Claude-powered risk agents, and a cross-platform Vue/Capacitor client.

Alongside it I built calibrated sports-prediction models and a from-scratch quant risk toolkit. I treat model output as something to bound, not blindly trust, and I care most about systems that have to be correct under real money and real adversaries. Finance degree, pricing & valuation emphasis (UGA, magna cum laude).
